## Pricing Rule (Mandatory)

Today's Rate = Current live exchange rate from EUR to INR.

If Original Price < €1,000

Final Price = ((Original Price × (Today's Rate + 12)) + 2000) × 1.25

If Original Price ≥ €1,000

Final Price = ((Original Price × (Today's Rate + 12)) + 2000) × 1.22

For €1,365, the multiplier **must be 1.22**.

## Investigate These Possible Causes

* Compare-at (RRP) price used instead of sale price.
* Currency converted twice.
* Incorrect exchange rate.
* +12 added more than once.
* ₹2,000 added more than once.
* Wrong multiplier applied.
* Threshold logic incorrect.
* Hidden markup or pricing rule applied after calculation.
* Shopify modifying the price after upload.
* Multiple pricing engines producing different values.
