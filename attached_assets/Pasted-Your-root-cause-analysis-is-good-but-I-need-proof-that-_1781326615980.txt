# Pricing Rules (Mandatory)

Every product must be converted to **Indian Rupees (INR)** before publishing to Shopify.

Use the **latest live exchange rate** of the source currency to INR.

Supported currencies include, but are not limited to:

* EUR
* USD
* GBP
* CHF
* AUD
* CAD
* JPY
* CNY
* HKD
* SGD
* AED
* SAR
* SEK
* NOK
* DKK
* NZD
* Any additional source currency

No currency may fall back to another currency's exchange rate. If a live INR rate cannot be retrieved, stop processing and report an error.

## Pricing Formula

Today's Rate = Current live exchange rate from the source currency to INR.

For every supported currency:

If Original Price < 1000:

Final Price = ((Original Price × (Today's Rate + 12)) + 2000) × 1.25

If Original Price ≥ 1000:

Final Price = ((Original Price × (Today's Rate + 12)) + 2000) × 1.22

The final Shopify selling price must exactly equal this calculation.
