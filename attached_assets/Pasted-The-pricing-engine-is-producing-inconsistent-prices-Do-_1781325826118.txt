The pricing engine is producing inconsistent prices. Do not patch individual products. Find and fix the root cause.

## Objective

Audit the entire pricing pipeline from product scraping to Shopify publishing and determine exactly why the generated store prices do not match the pricing formula.

## Pricing Formula

Today's Rate = Current live exchange rate of the source currency to INR.

For every currency:

* Price < 1000
  Final Price = ((Original Price × (Today's INR Rate + 12)) + 2000) × 1.25

* Price ≥ 1000
  Final Price = ((Original Price × (Today's INR Rate + 12)) + 2000) × 1.22

The final store price must match this formula exactly.

## Audit Every Step

Trace the complete calculation pipeline and log the value after every step:

1. Original scraped price
2. Original currency
3. Exchange rate source
4. Live exchange rate retrieved
5. Timestamp of exchange rate
6. Exchange rate after adding +12
7. Price after conversion to INR
8. Price after adding ₹2,000
9. Applied multiplier
10. Final calculated price
11. Final Shopify price

## Check for These Bugs

* Incorrect exchange rate being fetched.
* Cached or stale exchange rate.
* Wrong currency mapped to the product.
* Currency converted twice.
* INR value converted again.
* +12 added multiple times.
* ₹2,000 added multiple times.
* Wrong multiplier selected.
* Threshold logic incorrect (<1000 vs ≥1000).
* Formula executed in the wrong order.
* Hidden shipping/import/tax markup added.
* Shopify modifying the price after upload.
* Price rounded before completing the calculation.
* Different pricing logic used for different stores or suppliers.
* Exchange rate API returning inverse rates.
* Currency symbols incorrectly parsed.

## Validation

Before publishing every product:

* Recalculate the price independently using the pricing formula.
* Compare the calculated value with the generated Shopify price.
* If the difference is greater than ₹1, stop publishing immediately.
* Print the exact step where the calculation diverges.
